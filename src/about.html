About

FCC Maps supports publication of geospatial visualizations related to reports, public notices, and other Federal Communications Commission activities. We focus on using tools and techniques that support rapid development and deployment of visualizations and their underlying data. Source code for most maps is publicly available on GitHub.

Since the first map was released in 2011, we have published numerous maps each year on topics ranging from enforcement actions against pirate radio to block-level broadband deployment by provider. We are excited that FCC Maps continues to grow as a service for the public to enhance understanding on key Commission items.
