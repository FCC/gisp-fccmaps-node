About

FCC GIS supports publication of geospatial visualizations related to reports, public notices, and other Federal Communications Commission activities. We focus on using tools and techniques that support rapid development and deployment of visualizations and their underlying data.

The FCC publishes numerous maps each year on topics ranging from nationwide LTE coverage to fixed broadband deployment data. We are excited that FCC GIS continues to grow as a service for the public to enhance understanding on key Commission items.

Offices and Bureaus

Consumer and Governmental Affairs
Develops and implements consumer policies including those for people with disabilities and those affecting tribal nations.
Enforcement
Enforces the Communications Act and FCC rules by protecting consumers, fostering efficient spectrum use, furthering public safety and promoting competition.
International
Administers international telecommunications and satellite programs and policies, including licensing and regulatory functions.
Media
Oversees broadcast radio and television, as well as cable and satellite services on behalf of consumers.
Public Safety & Homeland Security
Supports initiatives that strengthen public safety and emergency response capabilities.
Wireless Telecommunications
Regulates domestic wireless telecommunications programs and policies, including licensing.
Wireline Competition
Develops and recommends wireline telecommunications policy goals, objectives, programs and plans.
